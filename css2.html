Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) admission 2022 to PG Diploma is open now. The institute will conduct an entrance exam for PG Diploma admission on July 30 and 31, and the admit cards will be released between July 25 and July 30. The result of the entrance exam will be declared on August 10, followed by counselling from August 10 to August 16. Second counselling will be held from August 30 to September 3. Nov 10, 2022: C-DAC offering programmes to empower educators via social media: AICTERead More The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing, also termed C-DAC, is located in Pune, Maharashtra. The institute was established in 1993 by the Government of India as an autonomous institute under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. At first, the institute was established only as an R&D Centre but 20 years later they started offering courses starting with only 20 students. Institute also provides its services to various government bodies and armed forces by making and taking care of their websites. C-DAC has a huge network of training centres across the country. As of now, a total of 50 training centres are situated in Grade A cities, like Bengaluru, Mumbai, Chennai, Hyderabad, etc. The institute offers formal courses such as PG diploma, online diploma, Programme for Advancing Computer Education (PACE), MTech, MBA and MCA programmes.
